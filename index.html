About

I am an interdisciplinary scientist currently serving as an Assistant Professor at Krea University (2021–present). My research focuses on reconciling human development with planetary boundaries, drawing from a unique academic foundation in both theoretical physics and earth system science.

I hold dual doctorates: a Ph.D. in Climate Science from the Max Planck Institute for Biogeochemistry (2017) and a Ph.D. in Quantum Information from the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (2013). Before joining Krea, I was a Research Associate at the Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology (IITM) in Pune (2019–2021).

Research Interests

  • Global and regional monsoon projections
  • Environmental performance of countries
  • Climate responsibility and finance
  • Sustainable development
