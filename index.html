About

I am a climate and sustainability scientist, and currently an Assistant Professor of Environmental Studies at Krea University in India.

I hold dual doctorates: a Ph.D. in Climate Science from the Max Planck Institute for Biogeochemistry, Germany (2017) and a Ph.D. in Quantum Information and Communication from the Institute of Photonic Sciences, Barcelona, Spain (2013).

Before joining Krea, I was a Research Associate at the Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology (IITM) in Pune.

I was a contributing author to Atlas in IPCC AR6, and have led/co-authored (WG1 style) climate change assessments for India.

Research Interests

  • Global and regional monsoon projections
  • Environmental performance of countries
  • Climate responsibility and finance
  • Sustainable development
